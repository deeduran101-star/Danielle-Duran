## Key Sociological Theories & Concepts
Danielle frequently engages with the following theoretical frameworks:
- **Sociological Imagination:** Connecting personal troubles to public issues and structural forces.
- **Labeling Theory (Becker):** How institutions (schools, courts) label marginalized youth and how those labels become self-fulfilling or define individual trajectories.
- **Conflict Theory (Marx, Weber):** Analyzing power dynamics, inequality, and how institutions serve the interests of those in power.
- **Structural Bias & Inequality:** Examining race, class, and intersecting identities within institutional frameworks.
- **Institutional Analysis:** Critically evaluating bureaucracies, the justice system, and educational systems.
